Conclusion and Findings:

We found that there was a very wide range of requests when it came to creating an app for the outdoors. Among those, the pain point that was mentioned the most was the paywall that some apps utilize for monitization. Something like a "pro" version that give more features when compared to the average user. Some of our interviewees that are on the younger side (20-25) would prefer to have ads with the same features as the pro version ranther than paying for a subscription.

We also had mention of a trail review system that could give information on the trails in that area, something to warn people who want to visit a trail or place that something might be off. It would keep the trail information more up to date remotely so you can plan before you're even there.
